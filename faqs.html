Frequently Asked Questions

What are your store hours?

We are open from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM, including weekends and holidays.

Are your breads baked fresh daily?

All our breads and pastries are baked fresh every morning to ensure the best taste and quality.

Do you offer delivery services?

We currently offer delivery within Tagum City through selected delivery partners.

How much is the delivery fee?

We have a flat rate delivery fee of P100 within Tagum City areas.

What are the steps for delivery orders?
  1. To start, please browse our menu online or through our social media page and choose the items you’d like.
  2. Once you’ve decided, place your order by sending us a message, giving us a call, or ordering through our delivery app if available.
  3. Kindly provide your complete delivery address and contact number so we can arrange the delivery smoothly.
  4. We will confirm the availability of your items, the total price, and the estimated delivery time.
  5. After confirmation, you may choose your preferred payment method such as cash on delivery, bank transfer, e-wallet, or app payment.
  6. Our rider or courier partner will deliver your order to your address at the agreed time.
Do you have gluten-free options?

We offer a limited selection of gluten-free products. Please ask our staff for available items.

How do I store Komorebi baked goods?

For breads, it is best consumed within the day of purchase but it can be stored in the chiller up to 2 days. For cakes, you may store it in the chiller for 2-3 days or in the freezer for up to 7 days. For pastries, you may store it in the chiller for 2-3 days.
